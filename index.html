About Us

AITCH Technologies LLC

AITCH Technologies LLC is the patent holding and licensing division of AITCH Holdings LLC. We specialize in developing innovative sustainable technologies and licensing them to institutional partners worldwide.

Our portfolio of ten patent-holding entities covers construction, agriculture, and energy sectors. Each entity holds patents for specific technologies designed to reduce environmental impact while providing commercial value.

We work with manufacturers, developers, and government agencies to bring our sustainable technologies to market through flexible licensing arrangements.
